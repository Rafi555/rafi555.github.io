Worlds Apart: Montreg & Sardeen

The narrative unfolds in a fractured world, starkly divided into two realms: Montreg, the gleaming city of the privileged scientific community, and Sardeen, the desolate land of the exploited. This section allows you to explore the profound contrasts that define their existence and sow the seeds of conflict.

Montreg: The Gilded Cage

A beacon of supposed progress and prosperity, Montreg is where the architects of this world reside. Life is a symphony of comfort, intellectual pursuit, and chilling detachment from the suffering that fuels their ease.

Clean air, abundant fresh food, advanced healthcare, and sophisticated entertainment define daily life. Resources are plentiful, flowing seamlessly from Sardeen.
They are the undisputed rulers, driven by logic, efficiency, and a belief in their inherent superiority. Ethical considerations are often secondary to "progress" and control.

Sardeen: The Crushing Weight

In stark contrast, Sardeen is a land of despair. Its people toil under Montreg's shadow, their lives defined by hardship, scarcity, and the constant threat of their overlords.

Existence is a battle for meager rations. The land is tired, resources are dwindling, and much of what is produced is siphoned off to Montreg.
The people are hardened by generations of oppression, yet flickers of defiance and a deep-seated resilience persist amidst the pervasive despair.

The Yoke of Oppression

Montreg's control over Sardeen is absolute and multifaceted, maintained through systematic exploitation and brutal enforcement. This section details the mechanisms of oppression that fuel Montreg's prosperity and Sardeen's suffering, ultimately leading to the breaking point.

Resource Exploitation: Sardeen's Burden

Sardeen's primary role is to serve as Montreg's breadbasket and resource mine. The chart below illustratively depicts the one-sided flow of resources.

The Crushing Tax

A brutal system of taxation forces Sardeen to surrender the majority of its food and vital resources to Montreg. This ensures Montreg's abundance while keeping Sardeen perpetually on the brink of starvation, a key tool of control.

The Vanishing People

Montreg's enforcers conduct raids at will, abducting individuals for slave labor in Montreg or, more chillingly, for "research" in their advanced labs. Few ever return, their fates grimly unknown or whispered in fear.

Instruments of Betrayal

Some abducted from Sardeen are twisted into tools of their oppressors. "Promoted" to soldiers, they return to their homeland with cold eyes and colder hearts, enforcing Montreg's brutal regime upon their own people, a deeply insidious form of psychological warfare.

The First Shield: A Spark of Defiance

From the depths of despair and suffering, a flicker of organized resistance was born. The First Shield represents Sardeen's desperate, defiant stand against Montreg's tyranny. This section explores their emergence and what they fight for.

The Birth of Resistance

Forged in secret by Sardeen's most resilient, the First Shield is a clandestine stronghold and a symbol of hope. They are the last line of defense against total annihilation and the embodiment of the will to survive.

Their goals are simple yet monumental: to protect their people, to reclaim their dignity, and to fight for a future where Sardeen is free from Montreg's grasp. They are outnumbered and outgunned, but their spirit is unbroken.

The Anomaly: Subject 7

In Montreg's sterile labs, a new horror was being engineered: clones derived from "strong genes" of abducted Sardeen individuals, designed for the genocide of the First Shield. But the meticulous plans of the scientific community went awry. This section follows the story of one such clone, Subject 7.

The Escape & The Revelation

A glitch, an accident ‚Äì Subject 7 escaped the cloning facility. In his disoriented flight, he stumbled upon the horrifying truth: his purpose, and that of his brethren, was to exterminate the very people from whom his genetic material was derived, the First Shield.

The Burden of Knowledge

Horror, an entirely new sensation, gripped him. He knew he had to warn them. But he carried a terrible burden: he looked exactly like one of Montreg's own. "My face is theirs, but my knowledge is Sardeen's only hope. How can they trust the image of their enemy?" His journey to reach the First Shield, to convince them of the impending doom, would be fraught with peril, his appearance a constant threat to his mission and his life.

A Perilous Path

Under the cloak of Montreg's oppressive night, Subject 7 slipped away, a ghost in the gleaming city, his only goal to reach the First Shield. Could he, a product of Montreg's darkest ambition, convince the very people he was designed to destroy of the impending doom? His journey had just begun.
